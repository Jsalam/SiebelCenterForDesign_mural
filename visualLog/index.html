May 2020
Initial concept

The visualization could take the form of a massive arc diagram or any other layout. I envision a wall populated with a large number of 3D printed objects inspired by the selected innovations, annotated with a brief description of what they represent, and arranged at eye-height level along the wall. The objects will be connected by color-coded lines of words drawn on the wall. application that visitors will use on their phones.

In addition, this flat layout would pop-out into the building space with the help of an augmented reality (AR). By using 3D objects as AR markers, the arcs will project themselves into the open space of the room, attracting the attention to the building structure and enticing the audiences to explore further the innovation network relative to the object. The projected arcs will be strings of ‘marching words’ describing the types of innovation present at U of I.
