SCD Mural

Intro

This document is the record of the development of a mural for the Siebel Center for Design at the University of Illinois.

The sketches and prototypes compiled are part of the thinking process of a creative team composed by a designer (Daniela Delgado), an architect (Faithful Oladeji), an anthropologist (Kristen Allen), lead by Juan Salamanca, Ph.D. We work closely with Nicholas Puddicombe, Associate Director of Operations and Experience, and Rachel Swiztky, Director of the Siebel Center for Design.
